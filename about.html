Our Rooms

A warm welcome awaits you.

We are a leading eco-tourism operator approved by the Ministry of Tourism, Government of Tamil Nadu, offering customized nature-based programs for families, corporates, and student groups. With over 10 years of experience, we provide guided treks in partnership with Trek Tamil Nadu, exploring designated forest routes and rich biodiversity. All treks are led by trained forest guides, ensuring safety, learning, and environmental responsibility. Participants are covered by trek insurance and must follow strict eco-friendly practices, including a “Leave No Trace” policy. Our sustainable programs support local communities while inspiring individuals to appreciate, protect, and conserve nature for future generations.
