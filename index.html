Eco Tourism

A Sustainable Journey into Nature

Nature Trails N Treks is a leading eco-tourism operator approved by the Ministry of Tourism, Government of Tamil Nadu. With over 10 years of experience, we specialize in creating sustainable and memorable travel experiences that connect people with nature.

In partnership with Trek Tamil Nadu (Tamil Nadu Wilderness Experiences Corporation), we provide guided trekking experiences through approved forest routes. These treks are led by trained forest department guides who ensure safety while educating participants about biodiversity, terrain, and conservation practices.
