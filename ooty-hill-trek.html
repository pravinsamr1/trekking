Ooty Hill Trek – Nilgiri Mountain Experience

Located in the heart of the :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0} (Udhagamandalam), this scenic hill trek offers a refreshing journey through the lush :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}. Experience rolling grasslands, dense forests, and breathtaking viewpoints as you explore one of South India’s most popular trekking destinations.

The trek passes through expansive tea estates, eucalyptus forests, and mist-covered trails leading to scenic viewpoints like :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2} — the highest peak in the Nilgiris. The terrain is a mix of gentle climbs and moderate slopes, making it suitable for beginners and nature lovers.

Along the way, you may encounter local tribal settlements and enjoy panoramic views of valleys and forests. The cool climate and fresh mountain air make this trek a rejuvenating escape from city life. Trek Information
  • Maximum Elevation: 2637 MSL
  • Forest Type: Shola Forest & Grasslands
  • Medical: 3 km

  • Railway: 2 km
  • Parking: Yes
  • Reception: Ooty Town
Notable Wildlife
  • Nilgiri Tahr
  • Indian Bison (Gaur)
  • Malabar Giant Squirrel

We follow a strict eco-friendly “Leave No Trace” policy to preserve the natural beauty of the Nilgiris. Our goal is to promote sustainable tourism while offering a safe and memorable trekking experience. Discover the charm of Ooty’s hills and reconnect with nature.
