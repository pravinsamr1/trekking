Kodaikanal Forest Trek

Kodaikanal Forest Trek – Vellagavi Village Experience

Just an hour’s drive from Kodaikanal, this scenic trek takes you deep into nature and traditional village life. Walk through lush coffee plantations, misty hills, and peaceful forest trails as you descend towards the hidden village of Vellagavi. This downhill trek offers a perfect mix of adventure, culture, and breathtaking landscapes.

The trek begins amidst serene greenery and continues downhill across rocky terrain, so sturdy footwear is essential. Don’t miss the breathtaking views from Dolphin’s Nose — one of the most iconic viewpoints in Kodaikanal. Though wildlife sightings are rare, the region is a paradise for birdwatchers and nature enthusiasts.

Your journey ends in the peaceful village of Vellagavi, where you can relax in local accommodations or camp near the forest. The villagers’ tradition of walking barefoot reflects their deep connection with nature, offering you a truly authentic rural experience. You may also choose to extend your journey to the stunning Kumbakarai Waterfalls.
